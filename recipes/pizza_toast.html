Pizza Lava Toast

Description

A simple toast covered with melted cheese and toppings

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of butter
  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 1/2 ounces of sliced mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 an ounce of sliced pepper jack cheese
  • 5 slices of pickled jalapeno
  • 1 tablespoon of pizza sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Italian seasoning, divided
  • Crushed red pepper, to taste
  • Ground Black Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 235 Fahrenheit or 135 Degrees Celsius.
  2. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  3. Spread butter on both sides of each slice of bread and place on the prepared pan.
  4. Bake, flipping once halfway through, until bread is lightly browned on both sides, about 8 minutes.
  5. Cover the perimeter of one bread slice with half of the mozzarella cheese, leaving the middle part of the bread empty.
  6. Top with other slice of bread.
  7. Use the back of a spoon to make an indentation in the middle of the bread slices, pushing down firmly to reach the bottom slice.
  8. Add the sauce to the indentation, and sprinkle half the Italian seasoning over the top.
  9. Cover with remaining 1/2 ounce mozzarella cheese and pepper jack, followed by jalapeno and pepperoni.
  10. Garnish with remaining Italian seasoning, crushed red pepper and black pepper.
  11. Bake until the cheese is melted and golden-brown, about 5 minutes. Slice in half and serve.
