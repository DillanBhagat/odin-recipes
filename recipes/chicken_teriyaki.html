Chicken Teriyaki

Description

A plate of white rice, with chicken pieces covered in soy sauce, green onions and brocoli.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of sake
  • 3 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of white sesame seeds
  • 1/4 tablespoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 teaspoons of canola or peanut oil
  • 1 teaspoon of toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon finely minced garlic
  • 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked rice

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, sake, rice vinegar, brown sugar in a bowl and whisk well until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Stir in sesame seeds and crushed red pepper and set aside.
  3. Heat oils in a large skillet over high heat.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper evenly over chicken. Add chicken in a single layer to the skillet and cook, undisturbed until chicken is browned and releases easily from the pan, 4 to 6 minutes.
  5. Flip chicken pieces, cook 1 more minute and remove from pan;set aside.
  6. Reduce heat to medium and add ginger and garlic. Cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  7. Reserve 2 tablespoons of the green onions for garnish and add remaining green onions to the skillet.
  8. Cook for 1 minute. Add 1 cup reserved soy sauce mixture to the skillet and stir with a wooden spoon, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
  9. Bring mixture to a boil and cook until sauce has reduced by about a third or until slightly thickened and glossy.
  10. Return chicken to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thickened slightly and chicken is coated in the sauce and cooked through.
  11. Serve over rice and sprinkled with reserved green onions.
